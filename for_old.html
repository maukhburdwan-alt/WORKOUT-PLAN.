Exercise & Diet Plan for Older Adults (60+)

Exercise Plan

Warm-up (5 minutes)

  • Slow marching in place – 2 min
  • Shoulder rolls – 1 min
  • Neck rotations – 1 min
  • Ankle rotations – 1 min

Strength Training (10–12 minutes)

  • Upper body: Wall push-ups, seated dumbbell/water bottle press, bicep curls
  • Lower body: Sit-to-stand, heel raises, side leg lifts

Balance (3–5 minutes)

  • One-leg stand – 20–30 sec each side
  • Tandem walk – 1 min
  • Side stepping – 1 min

Flexibility (5 minutes)

  • Hamstring stretch – 15–20 sec
  • Chest stretch – 15–20 sec
  • Shoulder stretch – 15–20 sec
  • Calf stretch – 15–20 sec

Optional Light Cardio (10 minutes)

Walking, slow cycling, or low-impact aerobics.

Diet Plan

Morning

Warm water (optional: honey or lemon)

Breakfast

  • Oats with fruits and nuts
  • Poha or upma
  • 2 boiled eggs + fruit
  • Dalia

Lunch

  • 1–2 chapatis
  • 1 bowl dal
  • 1 bowl vegetables
  • Curd
  • Optional: 1/2 bowl brown rice

Evening Snacks

  • Green tea or lemon tea
  • Roasted chana
  • Fruit (apple, banana, or papaya)

Dinner

  • 1–2 chapatis or vegetable soup
  • Vegetable sabji
  • Dal or paneer

Hydration

6–8 glasses of water. Coconut water or buttermilk optional.

Safety Tips

  • Avoid fast movements or heavy weights
  • Stop if chest pain, dizziness, or breathlessness occurs
  • Keep a chair or wall for support
