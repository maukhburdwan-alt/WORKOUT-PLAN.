General Fitness & Exercise Guide

Note: This is a basic fitness guide suitable for most healthy individuals. Make sure to warm up, stay hydrated, and progress slowly.

Fitness Goals

  • Improve overall strength
  • Build stamina and endurance
  • Increase flexibility and mobility
  • Maintain a healthy body composition

Weekly Workout Overview

A balanced fitness routine includes strength training, cardio, and mobility work.

Sample 5-Day Plan

  • Day 1 — Strength Training (Upper Body)
    • Push-ups — 3 x 10–12
    • Dumbbell Rows — 3 x 12 each side
    • Shoulder Press — 3 x 10
    • Tricep Dips — 3 x 12
    • Plank — 30–45 sec
  • Day 2 — Cardio
    • Jogging / brisk walk — 20–30 min
    • Jump rope — 5 min (optional)
    • Cool down walk + stretches — 5 min
  • Day 3 — Strength Training (Lower Body)
    • Squats — 3 x 12–15
    • Lunges — 3 x 10 each leg
    • Glute Bridges — 3 x 15
    • Calf Raises — 3 x 20
    • Side Plank — 20–30 sec each side
  • Day 4 — Flexibility & Mobility
    • Full-body stretching — 15–20 min
    • Yoga or light mobility flow — 15–20 min
  • Day 5 — Full Body Circuit
    • Repeat this circuit 3 times:
    • 10 Push-ups
    • 15 Squats
    • 10 Rows
    • 20 Mountain Climbers
    • 30-second Plank
  • Day 6 — Optional Light Activity
    • Walking, cycling or swimming — 20–30 min
  • Day 7 — Rest
    • Complete rest or gentle stretching

General Fitness Diet Tips

  • Eat balanced meals with protein, carbs, and healthy fats.
  • Stay hydrated — drink 6–10 glasses of water daily.
  • Include fruits and vegetables in every meal.
  • Limit junk food, sugary drinks, and overly processed snacks.
  • Eat enough protein to support muscle recovery.

Sample Daily Diet

  • Breakfast: Eggs or oats + fruit + a handful of nuts
  • Mid-morning: Fruit or yogurt
  • Lunch: Rice/roti + dal/chicken + vegetables + salad
  • Evening snack: Roasted chana, nuts, or fruit
  • Dinner: Light meals — soup, vegetables, lean protein
  • Optional: Herbal tea before bed

Safety Tips

  • Warm up before every workout.
  • Cool down and stretch afterward.
  • Increase intensity slowly.
  • Stop if you feel dizziness, chest pain, or sharp pain.

Stay active, stay consistent, and enjoy your fitness journey!
